Software Engineering Career Presentation

Who am I?

Why am I here?

  • Talk about Software Engineering as a career AND for fun!
  • To get you excited about programming (so that you'll all want to work as engineers someday)

Where do I work?

Cerner

What does Cerner do?

We create healthcare-related software for doctors/nurses and patients.

Wait... What?

Healthcare software = Computer programs for:

  • Collecting patient info (name, date of birth)
  • Scheduling appointments
  • Tracking medications
  • Notifying doctors/nurses of patient health issues
  • Much, much, more...

What do Software Engineers do?

  • Design program behaviors and appearance
  • Break logic into steps a computer can understand
  • Use programming languages to instruct computers
  • Write, review, and test LOTS of code
  • ...and perhaps most importantly...

Work with their teams to solve problems!!!

(Computer programmers still have to talk to people!)

What's the Work Environment Like?

  • Creative / Fun
  • Relaxed Dress Code
  • Teamwork Required!

Why Should you Care about Software Engineering?

  • Computer programs are involved in nearly all aspects of our lives
  • Lets you use your brain and creativity to create anything you can imagine
  • TONS of great jobs looking for smart people like you!

What if you want to be something else when you grow up?

Learn to program anyway so you can:

  • Show off your art, music, writing, or other hobbies
  • Create websites or mobile apps for your own business
  • Make games for fun (or money)
  • Make the world work the way YOU want

Truths about Software Engineering

In case you have doubts, let's clear up the following:

  • Programming is for girls and boys
  • You don't have to love or even be good at math or science to be successful
  • Programming takes practice and study to get better (just like math, music, sports, etc)
  • Nobody knows it all. We try, we fail, we learn, we try again. We improve over time.
